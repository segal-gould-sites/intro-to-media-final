Enter text that you expect to sound like Hillary Clinton, Donald Trump, Bernie Sanders, Gary Johnson, or Jill Stein during their individual campaigns for president of the United States.

This page will output the probability found through a Naive Bayes Classifier and a Stochastic Gradient Descent Classifier which were trained on the tweets shared by each candidate during their individual campaigns that the text you choose to input resembles those tweets. It also uses Latent Dirichlet Allocation to build a topic model of all tweets from a candidate as well as your input.

Follow @intro_to_media to see users' (uncensored) queries.
